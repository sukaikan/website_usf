About Us

Welcome to The Cat Corner, the official Medium blog of Universitas Padjadjaran Street Feeding Animal Friend (UnpadSF). UnpadSF is a charity initiative founded by senior students of Faculty of Medicine and Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences in 2021 for homeless community cats & dogs and promoting responsible pet ownership among students.

UnpadSF aims to:

  • Provide humane care for stray cats and dogs, ensuring a healthy environment.
  • Implement a Trap-Neuter-Return-Maintain (TNRM) program for population control.
  • Educate the community about the role of free roaming cats and dogs in the ecosystem.

UnpadSF activities range from stray animal feeding, vaccination, rescues, Trap-Neuter-Return program, campaign, cat adoption programs & developing reflective collar as a product to promote pet safety.

We're proud to be a part of creating a more compassionate culture and to continue building a better world for both human & animals.

Universitas Padjadjaran Street Feeding Animal Friend (UnpadSF) is based in Universitas Padjadjaran Jatinangor campus, Sumedang, Indonesia. UnpadSF also promotes responsible pet cat & dog ownership and uses both English and Indonesian to reach a broader audience.
